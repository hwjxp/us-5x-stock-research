# Output Template

## 1. 5x Potential Scorecard

Use this structure for a current stock:

- `Conclusion`: one-paragraph verdict
- `Regime Fit`: what market backdrop helps or hurts
- `Theme`: why this theme can or cannot support a 5x outcome
- `Leader Quality`: why this name is or is not the right vehicle
- `Catalysts`: next 3 to 12 months
- `Market-Cap Fit`: why upside is or is not mathematically plausible
- `Technical Setup`: chart archetype, key levels, buy/add/avoid zones
- `Invalidation`: what breaks the thesis

## 2. Historical Analog Review

- `Closest analogs`
- `Shared features`
- `Critical differences`
- `What would need to happen for this stock to follow them`

## 3. Trade Plan

- `Starter zone`
- `Add zone`
- `Do-not-chase zone`
- `Risk level`
- `Exit / trim triggers`

## 4. Candidate Ranking

When ranking multiple names, return:

- `Top tier`
- `Watchlist tier`
- `Too early / too risky`

Always state:

- whether the view is based on current live data or historical comparison
- where the thesis is strongest
- where the thesis can fail quickly
