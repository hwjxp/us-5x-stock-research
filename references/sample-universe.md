- `SNDK`
  Treat as a spinoff-era special case. Sandisk completed separation from Western Digital and began trading independently on `2025-02-24`, so 2025 performance is not directly comparable with a normal full-calendar-year sample.

## Handling Rule

When using analogs:

1. Pick only names that match the current stock's business type and chart archetype.
2. Do not compare a steady institutional trend to a squeeze-driven meme move unless the user explicitly wants that contrast.
3. Mark special-case samples before drawing conclusions from them.
