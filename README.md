# US 5x Stock Research

A Codex skill for studying U.S. stocks through a `5x-10x` multi-bagger framework.

## What This Skill Does

This skill is built for users who want a repeatable process to:

- find potential `5x` to `10x` U.S. stock opportunities
- separate strong stock ideas from weak stock ideas
- identify better entry, add, trim, and exit points
- compare current candidates against historical multi-baggers

It is optimized for:

- regime analysis
- theme selection
- leader identification
- catalyst mapping
- market-cap and float fit
- technical entry and exit logic
- position sizing and trade management

## Repository Structure

- `references/earnings-playbook.md`: earnings setup and reaction framework

## Core Philosophy

The skill follows this sequence:

`regime -> theme -> leader -> catalyst -> sizing -> technical execution`

The emphasis is not on finding random cheap stocks.

The emphasis is on finding stocks where the market may still be underestimating:

- the size of the opportunity
- the speed of the business inflection
- the quality of the leader
- the durability of the trend

## Typical Use Cases

- `Screen the best U.S. stocks with 5x potential over the next 12 months`
- `Analyze whether this stock is a real multi-bagger candidate or just a hot story`
- `Rank these 20 names using a 5x potential scorecard`
- `Review the buy point and sell strategy for this stock`
- `Build an earnings reaction plan before results`
